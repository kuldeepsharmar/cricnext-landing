The Application collects information when you download and use it. This information may include:

  • Your device's Internet Protocol address (e.g. IP address)

User Accounts and Profile Data

To provide a personalized experience and maintain our global leaderboards, the Application utilizes an authentication system. When you log in (e.g., via Google Sign-In), we collect:

This information is used to display your standing on public leaderboards and to generate personalized, shareable prediction success cards (Flex Cards) that feature your chosen name and image.
